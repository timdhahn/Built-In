# Built-In Closet Components

## Structural / Enclosure
- Side panels (walls/towers)
- Top panel / ceiling panel
- Base / kickboard / toe kick
- Backing (drywall wall or ¼" ply)
- Face frames
- Doors (swing, sliding, bi-fold, no door)

## Shelving
- Fixed shelves (wood, plywood, melamine, glass)
- Adjustable shelves (shelf pins, standards/tracks)
- Corner shelves (pie-cut, lazy susan, angled)
- Slanted shoe shelves
- Shelf edging / lip (prevents items falling)
- Shelf lighting (LED strip underneath)

### Materials
- Solid wood
- Plywood
- Melamine
- Glass

### Backing Options
- Use the wall (no backing)
- ¼" plywood backing

## Hanging / Rods
- Single hang (long clothes – dresses, coats)
- Double hang (short clothes – shirts, jackets stacked)
- Valet rod (pull-out, temporary hang)
- Pants rack (pull-out, tiered)
- Belt/tie rack (pull-out or door-mounted)
- Scarf/accessory hooks

## Drawers
- Standard drawers (soft-close, dovetail)
- Deep drawers (sweaters, jeans)
- Shallow drawers (jewelry, accessories)
- Velvet-lined jewelry drawer
- Felt-lined watch/ring insert drawer
- False drawer fronts

## Pull-outs & Inserts
- Pull-out baskets (wire or solid)
- Laundry hamper pull-out
- Ironing board pull-out
- Trouser pull-out (clamp style)
- Slide-out mirror
- Pull-out dog leash/bag hooks

## Shoe Storage
- Flat shoe shelves
- Angled shoe shelves
- Shoe cubbies / boxes
- Pull-out shoe trays
- Boot storage (tall open space)
- Shoe carousel (corner unit)

## Specialty Cabinets
- Glass-front cabinets (display, bags, hats)
- Solid cabinet doors (concealed storage)
- Locked cabinet (jewelry, valuables)
- Hat shelf / hat boxes area
- Bag hooks / purse shelf with dividers

## Hardware & Accessories
- Shelf pins & standards (Häfele, Rev-A-Shelf)
- Soft-close drawer slides (undermount, side-mount)
- Push-to-open mechanisms
- Handles / knobs / finger pulls
- Mirror (full-length, inside door, pull-out)
- Interior lighting (LED puck, motion sensor, strip)
- Power outlet / USB charging strip
- Label holders

## Finishing Details
- Crown molding (top)
- Base molding
- Scribe molding (wall gaps)
- Paint, stain, or laminate finish
- Veneer edge banding
